Team

Candid is founded by Bindu Reddy and Arvind Sundararajan.

Bindu Reddy

CEO & Co-founder

An avid social media user from the days of LiveJournal, Bindu is also the CEO and Co-Founder of MyLikes Inc. Before MyLikes, Bindu was at Google, where she was the Product Lead for Google Docs and Blogger. While there she also launched an initial version of Google's social product which evolved to become Google Plus. Prior to Google, she was an early employee at Elance. She has a MS from Dartmouth and a BTech from IIT Bombay.

Arvind Sundararajan

CTO & Co-founder

Arvind is also the CTO and Co-Founder of MyLikes, Inc. Before MyLikes, Arvind was at Google where he was the backend team lead for the initial GMail team. He then went on to lead the AdSense targeting and serving backend. He has a MS in Computer Science from Stanford and a B.Tech in Computer Science from IIT Bombay.
