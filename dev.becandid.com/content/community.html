Community Guidelines

We want to make sure Candid is a place where everyone can speak their mind freely, not just a select few. Open, interesting and candid discourse is only possible with moderation.

Here are Candid's rules:

Spam

Please do not contact Candid users for commercial purposes without their prior consent. Interrupting Candid with repetitive messages or other activity which is disruptive is strictly prohibited. Spammy posts include meaningless or incomprehensible language. Flooding, spamming and intentionally avoiding the spam filter is NOT permitted. Use of scrapers and other automated techniques is not allowed.

Solicitation and Advertising

We do not permit solicitation and blatant self-promotion of your social media accounts and commercial goods and services. Any sort of advertising including referral links is not permitted.

Legal

You cannot post anything that is illegal in your country or in the United States.

Porn And Nudity

It is really simple: Apple and Google have strict guidelines against porn. So posting porn is not allowed.

Personal Information

You cannot post or request personal information.

Quality

The quality of posts is extremely important to this community. Contributors are encouraged to provide high-quality and informative content. Low-quality posts and comments will be filtered. There is a a specific group "Random Uncensored" where content is not filtered.

Posts Should Be On Topic

The purpose of Candid is to promote honest conversations. Your posts may be filtered or moved to Random if you post has nothing to do with the group you are posting in.

Hate Speech

We do not allow content that advocate against groups of people based on their race or ethnic origin, religion, disability, gender, age, nationality, veteran status, sexual orientation, or gender identity. On non-mobile versions of Candid, the group Random Uncensored is available for you to post legally permitted content. Of course, slander and libel laws apply, and posts which violate them are prohibited.

Child Endangerment

Candid has a zero-tolerance policy against child sexual abuse imagery. This is illegal and absolutely not permitted on Candid.

Complaints About Candid

Like other platforms such as 4chan, complaining about Candid (its policies, moderation, etc) may result in post deletion and a ban. Constructive positive or negative feedback about Candid can be posted to the group Candid Feedback. This rule does not apply to the Random Uncensored group.Impersonating Candid mods is not allowed. Mod posts will have the mod badge displayed.

Threats and Harassment

Making physical threats and/or harassing users is not permitted on Candid.

Graphic Content

Candid does not allow sharing images or messages that celebrate or glorify violence or cruelty.

Intellectual Property

Please be sure you have the right to share content on Candid before posting it. Candid is required to remove images or other content which violates copyright, trademark, or other legal rights.

Regulated Goods or Illegal Activity

Candid does not permit transactions involving regulated goods, including firearms, tobacco, alcohol or adult products. Additionally, Candid does not allow illegal activity on the platform.

Security

We actively prevent attempts to compromise our user's privacy or security, including those that use fraud or deception. Releasing the private information of members or non-members is strictly prohibited.

Reporting Abuse

Candid has many layers of protection for our users, but sometimes mistakes happen. If you notice something anywhere on Candid that you believe violates our terms, please report it by clicking on the report/block button on the dropdown next to the user's name assigned to that post. It is important to remember, however, that reporting a piece of content does not guarantee that it will be removed or filtered from Candid. Falsely reporting posts is a violation of our Community Standards.

Repeated violation of these rules will get you banned.
